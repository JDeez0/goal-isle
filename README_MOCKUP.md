## 🎮 How to Use

### **Explore Isles:**
1. Click any isle card to see details
2. View tasks and progress
3. Click "✓ Fill" to complete tasks
4. Watch mass increase in real-time

### **Create New Isles:**
1. Click the "✨ Spark" button
2. Enter a name for your isle
3. Choose an emoji
4. Start building your new goal!

### **Navigate:**
- Click cards to open modals
- Click "×" or outside modal to close
- Use back button if needed
