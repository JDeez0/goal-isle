Spark Details

What you see when you tap a spark on Home. The recipe / equation IS the completion-status visualization: dependency emojis sit above the =, the main emoji is the hero below; each grey→fills as its condition is met, and the hero lights when all deps are satisfied (or, for a no-dep spark, when the main emoji itself appears in chat). Icons lead the actions — no noun headers. Settings is creator-only.
